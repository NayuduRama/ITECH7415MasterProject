Sit-Ups to Twist

LLie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat to the floor. Place your hands behind your head, engage your core and do a full sit-up. At the top of the sit-up, bring your right elbow to your left knee and twist your body toward that side. Lower back down to start. Repeat this movement alternating sides each time.
